1. Introduction

Compass Solutions ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Real Estate AI platform and Propel Dialer software (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our Service.

This Policy applies to: (a) information about you as a Service user ("User Data"); and (b) information about third-party contacts you manage through the Service ("Contact Data"). You are the data controller for all Contact Data you upload or process through the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Account Data

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted/hashed form)
  • Professional license information (if provided)
  • Phone number(s) used with the Service
  • Billing and payment information (processed by third-party payment processors)

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use the Service, including call logs and duration, login timestamps and IP addresses, feature usage patterns, and device/browser information.

2.3 Contact Data You Provide

In the course of using the Service, you may upload or input personal information about your real estate contacts, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, property information, call notes, and pipeline status. You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the legal right to collect, store, and use this Contact Data.

2.4 Call Recordings

The Service may facilitate call recording where permitted by law. You are responsible for complying with all applicable call recording and wiretapping laws, including notifying all parties and obtaining required consent.

2.5 Voicemail Content

Pre-recorded voicemail messages you create and upload are stored on our servers for use with the automated voicemail drop feature.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the Service; process your calls and communications; generate AI-powered call scripts; send service-related notifications; respond to support requests; monitor for security threats; comply with legal obligations; and enforce our Terms of Service.

✓ We do NOT sell your personal information or Contact Data to third parties.
✓ We do NOT use your Contact Data to market our own services to your contacts.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate the Service, including:

  • Twilio Inc. — telephony, call routing, and SMS infrastructure
  • Supabase — database hosting and storage
  • Render — cloud hosting infrastructure
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-generated call scripts and agent responses
  • SendGrid — email delivery

These providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing their services to us.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, property, or safety.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information is subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain User Account Data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter. Contact Data is retained as long as you use the Service and for up to 30 days after termination. Call logs and recordings may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with real estate record-keeping requirements.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS), encrypted storage of sensitive credentials, and access controls. However, no method of Internet transmission is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
